    1. Mammography: A mammogram is an x-ray of the breast. It is the best screening test for breast cancer, and can find cancers when they are too small to feel.     2. ACS: The American Cancer Society recommends that women at average risk of breast cancer start mammograms at age 45. Women at high risk of breast cancer may need to start mammograms earlier.

    3. USPSTF: The United States Preventive Services Task Force recommends that women ages 50 to 75 get a mammogram every two years. Some women may want to start mammograms at age 40.
