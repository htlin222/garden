# Assess severity-initial management-gastrointestinal bleeding

* VS including orthostatic Δs, JVP.
* Tachycardia (can be masked by βB use) suggests 10% volume loss,
* orthostatic hypotension 20% loss, shock >30% loss.
## Scoring predicts rebleeding & mortality:
* AIMS65,
* ABC Score
* [[Glasgow-Blatchford]].
