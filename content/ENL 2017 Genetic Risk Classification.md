## ENL 2017 Genetic Risk Classification
 (_Blood_ 2017;129:424)

**Risk Category**

**Genetic Abnormality**

Favorable

_APL_: t(15;17); _PML-RARa;_

t(8;21): _RUNX_1_-RUNX_1_T_1; inv(16): _CBFB-MYH_1;

mutated _NPM_1 _w/o FLT3-ITD or w/ FLT3-ITDlow_*;

biallelic mutation in _CEBPA_

Intermediate

_FLT3-ITD_low*; mutated _NPM_1 & _FLT3-ITD_high*; t(9;11): _MLL-MLLT3_;

cytogenetic abnl not classified as favorable or adverse, including normal karyotype w/o mutations in _FLT3-ITD_ & _NPM_1

Adverse

-5 or del(5q); -7; -17/abn(17p); complex or monosomal karyotype;

t(6;9): _DEK-NUP2_1_4_; t(9;22) _BCR-ABL_1; inv(3): _GATA2-MECOM_;

wildtype _NPM_1 & _FLT-ITDhigh_*; mutated _TP53, RUNX_1_, ASXL_1

* low/high: _FLT3-ITD_ variant allele frequency (VAF); reflects burden of mut. leukemic cells
