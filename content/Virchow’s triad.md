s triad

> s.i.t: statis, injury, thrombophilia

* Stasis:
	* bed rest,
	* inactivity
	* CHF
	* CVA w/in 3 mo
	* air travel >6 h.
* Injury to endothelium:
	* trauma
	* surgery
	* prior DVT
	* inflam
	* central catheter.
* Thrombophilia: genetic disorders (qv),
	* HIT
	* OCP
	* HRT
	* [[tamoxifen]]
	* raloxifene
