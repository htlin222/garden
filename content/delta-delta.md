# delta-delta

* (Δ/Δ = ΔAG/ΔHCO3) to assess if there is an additional metabolic

acid-base disturbance; ΔAG = (calculated AG – expected AG), ΔHCO3 = (24 – HCO3)
Δ/Δ = 1–2 → pure AG metabolic acidosis
Δ/Δ <1 → AG metabolic acidosis and simultaneous non-AG acidosis
Δ/Δ >2 → AG metabolic acidosis and simultaneous metabolic alkalosis
For pure **lactic acidosis Δ/Δ 1.6** b/c of slow lactate clearance

## Explain by ChatGPT

- To assess if there is an additional metabolic acid-base disturbance, the delta-delta method is used.
- Delta AG is calculated by taking the difference between the calculated AG and the expected AG.
- Delta HCO3 is calculated by taking the difference between 24 and the HCO3.
- A delta-delta of 1-2 indicates a pure AG metabolic acidosis.
- A delta-delta of less than 1 indicates an AG metabolic acidosis and a simultaneous non-AG acidosis.
- For pure lactic acidosis, the delta-delta is 1.6 because lactate is cleared slowly.
