# Laboratory Abnormalities of HBV Clinical Presentation

*   Transaminase elevation: 1/3 normal transaminase levels, only 25% have > 2x ULN, poor correlation between transaminase elevation and liver history.
*   Otherwise, no specific lab abnormalities except when cirrhotic or hepatic decompensation: low albumin, elevated bilirubin, AST/ALT ratio >1, low platelet count, cryoglobulinemia.
