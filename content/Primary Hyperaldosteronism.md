# Primary Hyperaldosteronism

1° hyperaldosteronism (eg, Conn’s)
(adrenal disorders, renin-independent increase in aldosterone; JCEM 2015;100:1) adrenal hyperplasia (60–70%), adenoma (Conn’s syndrome, 30–40%), adrenocortical cancer, glucocorticoid-remediable aldosteronism (GRA; ACTH-dep. rearranged promoter)
