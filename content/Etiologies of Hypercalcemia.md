↑ 1,25-(OH)2D (eg, rare lymphomas)

Hyperthyroidism, immobilization + Paget’s disease, vitamin A

scellaneous
Thiazides; Ca-based antacids or massive dairy consumption (milk-alkali syndrome); adrenal insufficiency
