# SOFA

## Sequential Organ Failure Assessment ([[SOFA]]):
↑ points for worsening organ dysfxn:

* respiration (↓ P:F ratio);
* coag (↓ plt);
* liver (↑ bili);
* CV (↓ MAP or ↑ pressors);
* CNS (↓ GCS);
* renal (↑ Cr or ↓ UOP)
