# Serologic and virologic tests

![image 22-10-01 20 57 27](https://i.imgur.com/mwATrDy.png)

* Precore mutant: HBeAg not made, but anti-HBe can develop due to x-reactivity w/ HBcAg; a/w ↑ HBV DNA

* HBsAg: appears before sx; used to screen blood donors; persists >6 mo = chronic HBV

* HBeAg: evidence of viral replication and ↑ infectivity

* IgM anti-HBc: 1st Ab to appear; indicates acute infection window period = HBsAg becomes ⊖, anti-HBs not yet ⊕, anti-HBc only clue to infxn

* IgG anti-HBc: indicates previous (HBsAg ⊖) or ongoing (HBsAg ⊕) HBV infection

* anti-HBe: indicates waning viral replication, ↓ infectivity

* anti-HBs: indicates resolution of acute disease & immunity (sole marker after vaccination)

* HBV DNA: presence in serum correlates w/ active viral replication in liver


## Explain by ChatGPT


* Serologic tests:

1. Precore mutant: HBeAg is not made, but anti-HBe can develop due to x-reactivity with HBcAg. This is associated with an increase in HBV DNA.

2. HBsAg: This appears before symptoms. It is used to screen blood donors. It persists.

3. HBeAg: This indicates viral replication and increased infectivity.

4. IgM anti-HBc: This is the first antibody to appear. It indicates the acute infection window period. During this time, HBsAg becomes negative, anti-HBs is not yet positive, and anti-HBc is the only clue to infection.

5. IgG anti-HBc: This indicates a previous (HBsAg negative) or ongoing (HBsAg positive) HBV infection.

6. Anti-HBe: This indicates waning viral replication and decreased infectivity.

7. Anti-HBs: This indicates resolution of acute disease and immunity (sole marker after vaccination).

8. HBV DNA: The presence of this in serum correlates with active viral replication in the liver.

* 血清學測試：

1. 前核心突變體：不產生 HBeAg，但由於與 HBcAg 的 x 反應，可產生抗 HBe。這與 HBV DNA 的增加有關。

2. HBsAg：出現在症狀之前。它用於篩查獻血者。它持續存在。

3. HBeAg：這表明病毒複製和傳染性增加。

4. IgM anti-HBc：這是最早出現的抗體。它表示急性感染窗口期。這段時間HBsAg轉陰，anti-HBs還未陽性，anti-HBc是感染的唯一線索。

5. IgG 抗-HBc：這表示先前（HBsAg 陰性）或正在進行（HBsAg 陽性）HBV 感染。

6. 抗-HBe：這表明病毒複製減弱和傳染性降低。

7. 抗-HBs：這表明急性疾病和免疫力的消退（接種疫苗後的唯一標誌）。

8. HBV DNA：它在血清中的存在與肝臟中活躍的病毒複製有關。
