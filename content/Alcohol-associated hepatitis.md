# Alcohol-associated hepatitis

* Sx: progressive jaundice, tender hepatomegaly, fever, ascites, GIB, encephalopathy

* Labs: ALT usually <300–500 w/ AST:ALT > 2:1, ↓ plt, ↑ Tbili & INR indicate severe hepatitis
