s C virus Monitoring on Rx

CBC, INR, LFTs, GFR, HCV VL prior to starting Rx. PIs contraindicated if decomp. liver dx (ascites, encephalopathy) or CPS ≥7. D/c Rx if jaundice, N/V, weakness, 10x ↑ in ALT, or significant ↑ in bili, Aϕ, INR after 4 wks.
