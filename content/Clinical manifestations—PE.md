title: Clinical manifestations—PE
---
stations—PE

* Dyspnea (~50%),
* pleuritic chest pain (~40%),
* cough (~23%),
* hemoptysis (~8%)

* ↑ RR (>70%)
* crackles (51%),
* ↑ HR (30%),
* fever,
* cyanosis,
* pleural friction rub,
* Loud P2 Pulmonary Hypertension

* Massive: syncope, HoTN, PEA; ↑ JVP, R-sided S3, Graham Steell (PR) murmur
