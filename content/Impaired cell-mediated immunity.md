# Impaired cell-mediated immunity

(CMI) (eg, HIV/AIDS, chronic steroids, posttransplant, DM, ESRD, autoimmune dis.)
Bacteria: Salmonella spp., Campylobacter, Listeria, Yersinia, Legionella (Lancet 2016;387:376), Rhodococcus, Nocardia, TB, non-TB mycobacteria
Fungi: Candida, Crypto, Histo, Coccidio, Aspergillus, Pneumocystis, Zygomycetes spp. and other molds
Viruses: HSV, VZV, CMV, EBV, JC virus, BK virus
Parasites: Toxoplasma, Cryptosporidium, Isospora, Microsporidia Babesia; Strongyloides
