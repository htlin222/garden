## relapse refractory of treatment of acute lymphoblastic leukemia (ALL)
salvage therapy (_below_), then allogeneic HSCT if able

_B cell:_ blinatumomab (CD19 BiTE-bispecific T-cell engager; NEJM2017;376:836), inotuzumab (CD22 Ab drug conjugate; NEJM2016;375:740); tisagenlecleucel and brexucabtagene autoleucel (CD19 CAR-T, NEJM 2018;378:449; Lancet 2021;398:491), TKI+chemo/steroids (Ph ⊕t(9;22) only)

_T cell:_ nelarabine ± cyclophosphamide and etoposide

_Both B & T cell:_ chemo including high dose cytarabine regimens; clofarabine
