# Alternative Diagnosis: Ischemic Bowel Secondary to Acute Mesenteric Ischemia or Ischemic Colitis

##### Ischemic Bowel

Three distinct clinical subtypes of ischemic bowel include chronic mesenteric ischemia (chronic small bowel ischemia), acute mesenteric ischemia (acute ischemia of small bowel), and ischemic colitis (ischemia of the large bowel). Chronic mesenteric ischemia is discussed at the end of the chapter.
